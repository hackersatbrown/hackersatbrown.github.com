Hackers at Brown

Event Sponsorship Options

We regularly host events for the Brown Computer Science undergrad community. To get in touch with us about events and sponsorships, please drop us a line at hackers@brown.edu

Any of the following are good ways to get in touch with CS students at Brown. If you have a different event idea, or would like to discuss one of these in more detail, please email us.

  • Tech Talk (basically a war story with a technology theme)
  • Tutorial (e.g. how to build a website)
  • Hackers & Painters (a mix of RISD designers and Brown CS students)
  • Dinner
  • Happy hour (your engineers mingle with CS students that are about to graduate)
  • Hackathon
  • Hack Friday

Topics we've hosted in the past that may inspire you:

  • A dramatic story about how your company had some kind of failure (downtime, PR, etc)
  • How to find people who cheat at poker on the internet
  • How to negotiate your inter/full-time offer
  • How to publicize your iphone app launch
  • How to write your resume
  • Design/UX/whatever
  • How to use git
  • Anti-rsi tips
  • Lockpicking
